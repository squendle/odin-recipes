Mushroom Rice Balls

This shit made Link salivate so it'll work for you too

Ingredients

  • Hylian Rice
  • Hylian Shroom
  • Rock Salt

Steps

  1. Stand by a cooking pot with lit fire underneath
  2. Go to Materials menu by clicking + button
  3. Hold Hylian Rice, Hylian Shroom, and Rock Salt
  4. Exit menu, and click A to Cook when prompted
  5. Listen to the fun music and enjoy!
